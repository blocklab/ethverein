About blockLAB

blockLAB Stuttgart is the network for blockchain technology in the Stuttgart region. The starting point of our cooperation is the mission statement of the "Blockchain Laboratory", a place for investigations and experiments around blockchain technology.
We believe that Blockchain technology will initiate a fundamental change in the Internet, which will have an impact on numerous industries and areas of life. We are convinced that the Stuttgart region with its industrial economic structure needs a blockchain network in order to be competitive in this key technology and to take advantage of the value creation opportunities that present themselves.

 

As the region's network for blockchain technology, we prepare information and make it available to interested parties. We inform and qualify topics on blockchain technology and network specialists and companies. In addition, we test manufacturer-independent and neutral existing offers, concepts and technologies and develop our own product and service ideas.

 

Our focus is on programming, business models and the Internet of Things (IoT).
